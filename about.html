Panda

Panda widows, orphans and elderly programme is a programme to support the vulnerable needy


Goal

  • To Transform the widows,Orphans and Elderly people life & change some of the Negative tradition practices

Vision

  • Cater for widpws, Orphans and Elderly people a long life,Dignity and Peaceful Death Through financial, economic and Spiritual support

  • To positively change/transform the saddened & suffering life of the widows, Orphans and Elders to Embrance long life,Dignity and peaceful Death

Mission

  • To help set the widows, Orpans and elderly people free from saddened & suffering life to Embrance long life . Dignity and peaceful death

  • To initiate programs and empower widows , Orphans and elderly people spiritually,socially and economically.
